    Home Delivered Meals

    Nutritious meals delivered to senior citizens in Gallia County. Hot meals delivered Monday-Thursday. Frozen meals delivered for Friday, Saturday and Sunday.

    Congregate Meal

    Provides socialization and hot meals served Monday-Thursday at Noon in the dining room.

    Home Care

    Provides personal care and home making services 7 days a week. Services include assistance with bathing, dressing, ambulating, cooking, light house cleaning, errands, and medical escort by trained nurse aides. Routine visits by a Registered Nurse.

    Transportation

    Provides transport for non-emergency medical appointments, physical therapy, dialysis, adult day service, Social Security Office, Job and Family Services, Community Action, other agencies and facilities, shopping, beauty salon, bank, post office, appointments outside of Gallia County. Monday-Friday.

    Mission Statement

    To establish and maintain a meeting place for the aged of Gallia County, Ohio and to facilitate the aged in recreational, handicraft, and other activities. To further the general education processes and to foster and promote planning services in assisting the aged throughout Gallia County, Ohio. To motivate and produce better living conditions for the aged of Gallia County, Ohio.
